About KDC

Kimberly Chaps, President of KDC Home Protection Services LLC and her family have been involved in the Real Estate/Property Management arena since 1990.

As a Licensed CAM (Community Association Manager), Licensed Realtor and Florida property investor her firm understands the high priority to protect your asset's value from harm. At KDC Home Protection Services we combine our extensive expertise and knowledge of area contractors and vendors to make sure we can find a quick remedy to any problems that may arise. The key to keeping your asset safe, is careful and thorough scheduled inspections and preventative measures.
We provide thorough property inspections in your absence, referrals to vetted professional service providers, key exchange services, open and closing "for season" visits, photo documentation for move out/move ins, and more.
